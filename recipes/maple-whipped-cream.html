Maple Whipped Cream

This is the most delicious cream you'll ever swallow.

It's very easy to make and super addicting, you'll be begging for more!

Ingredients
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1tbsp powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
Directions
  1. Place metal mixing bowl and metal whisk in freezer to cool
  2. Whisk cream in chilled bowl until frothy
  3. Add confectioner's sugar, maple syrup, and vanilla
  4. Continue to beat until soft peaks form
